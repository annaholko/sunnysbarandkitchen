About Sunny's

At Sunnys Bar & Kitchen, we offer Traditional homestyle curries in a beautiful country setting. We have had over 12 years experience in Dundee and the food is cooked by Sunny and his mum, from our oldest favourites like North Indian Garlic Chilli Chicken, or our newer Egyptian kebab. We now offer our own style of Steak Pies, Lasagne, Fish & Chips and other European dishes.
Book a table and you won’t be dissapointed.
