About Us

Sunny and his mum purchased their first takeaway 12 years ago on Peddie Street and since then have gained a lot of customers and experience. They used Sunny's late fathers recipe and built his dream to open a restaurant. Since then, they have produced a line of sauce (Baba's Sauce) which has been a very popular part of the business.


At Sunnys Bar & Kitchen we offer A la Carte but with European dishes added to the menu, we use local Produce and local butchers for all our food and have created a menu to cater for all. We take pride in our quality and we can guarantee good quality food at affordable prices.
